FAQ

What's the typical timeline for an AI implementation?

Most projects move through Assess (2–4 weeks), Design (3–6 weeks), Build (6–12 weeks), and Deploy (2–4 weeks). The exact timeline depends on complexity, data readiness, and team capacity. We provide detailed schedules during the planning phase.

Do you work with specific AI platforms or tools?

We remain platform-agnostic. Our recommendations are based on your requirements—whether that's OpenAI, Anthropic, open-source models, or proprietary solutions. We evaluate based on fit, cost, and risk, not vendor preference.

What if we don't have clean data?

Data quality issues are common. We audit your data during assessment and build data cleaning and preparation into implementation plans. Bad data is a known problem we solve for—it's not a blocker.

Can you help if we're just starting with AI?

Absolutely. That's why we start with assessment. If you're new to AI, we help you understand what's possible, what resources you need, and how to build capability internally. You won't be dependent on us forever—we build internal capacity.

How do you handle change management and team training?

Technical implementation is only part of the challenge. We work with your teams throughout, document processes, deliver training, and ensure people understand how to use and maintain the systems we build. Change management is part of every engagement.
