Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Proxiant Academy

Do I need prior AI or programming experience?

No! Our Fundamentals course is designed for complete beginners. However, the intermediate and advanced courses do assume basic familiarity with concepts covered in Fundamentals. We recommend starting with the appropriate level for your experience.

What's the time commitment for each course?

Time commitments vary by course format. Self-paced courses typically require 8-12 hours per week for their duration. Live cohort courses have scheduled class times plus assignments. We recommend dedicating 10-15 hours per week for optimal learning.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes! We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with a course for any reason within the first 14 days, we'll refund your full payment.

Is the PCAP certification recognized by employers?

Absolutely. The PCAP is recognized by leading enterprises and demonstrates professional competence in AI. Many of our graduates report it was valuable in job interviews and promotions.

What if I need to pause my course?

Self-paced courses offer lifetime access, so you can pause and resume whenever needed. For live cohort courses, we offer course deferrals to future cohorts if you need to pause.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans for most courses, allowing you to split the cost into monthly installments with no interest.

Can I audit a course without the certification?

Yes, we offer audit options for most courses at a reduced price. Audits give you access to all materials and lectures but don't include graded assignments or certification.

What if I have questions during the course?

All students have access to our community forum, live Q&A sessions, and instructor office hours. We're here to support your learning journey.
